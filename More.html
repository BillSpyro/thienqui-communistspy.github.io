Biography

I've lived in Sacramento, California, Salt Lake City, Utah, Chicago Illinoise and last but not least Lancaster, Pennsylvania where I still reside until this day and likely far into the near future. I attended Hamilton Elementary school, Wheatland Middle-School, and John Pierstol McCaskey's Highschool for my education. For my higher education I went to Thaddeus Steven's college, for Software engineering.
